ICT Club Terms and Conditions

1. Membership Obligations

  • Members must actively participate in all club activities, projects, and events.
  • Members must exhibit punctuality and ensure timely attendance at all meetings and events.
  • Members should complete all assigned tasks within the provided deadlines.

2. Code of Conduct

  • Members must maintain professionalism and ethics in all club-related communications and interactions.
  • Respect and courtesy must be shown to all club members, mentors, and guests.
  • Members must avoid discriminatory, harmful, or offensive behavior in any form.

3. ICT-Specific Guidelines

  • Members must adhere to ethical usage of technology and avoid unauthorized access to systems or data.
  • Members are required to protect and respect intellectual property rights, including software, codes, and creative work.
  • Members must ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations related to information technology and cybersecurity.

4. Contribution to the Club

  • Members should contribute innovative ideas and solutions to projects and discussions.
  • Members must foster a collaborative and supportive environment for learning and growth.
  • Sharing knowledge and mentoring junior members is highly encouraged.

5. Confidentiality

  • Members must safeguard sensitive or confidential information shared during club projects.
  • Unauthorized disclosure of club-related information is strictly prohibited.

6. Accountability

  • Members are accountable for the quality and integrity of their work.
  • Ownership must be taken for mistakes, and members should work to correct them promptly.

7. Membership Fees and Requirements

  • Members must pay any applicable membership fees on time, as decided by the club committee.
  • Members must maintain a valid and updated membership record with accurate details.

8. Breach of Terms

  • Violation of these terms and conditions may result in disciplinary actions or termination of membership.
  • Members are responsible for understanding and adhering to all rules and consequences set by the club.
