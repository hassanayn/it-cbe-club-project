About Our Club

Founded in 2025, CBE IT Club brings together students interested in technology, programming, cybersecurity, and digital innovation. We provide a platform for learning, collaboration, and professional growth.

Coding Workshops
Regular hands-on workshops covering web development, mobile apps, data science, and more.
Community
A supportive community of like-minded students sharing knowledge and experiences.
Tech Talks
Guest lectures from industry professionals and alumni working in tech.
Networking
Opportunities to connect with potential employers and internship providers.
